Everything is well and truly back to normal now and we hope you find something nice in our shop, named 'Gifted', this weekend!

On Wednesday, it being the first Wednesday of the month, our Bereavement Support Group will be offering hot drinks, biscuits and a friendly ear to all who need someone to chat with. The group be in the hall at 1030am until noon.

Other team members also remain busy with home and care home visits, the B-Attitudes and the food bank.

If any of these workstreams interest you, then why not reach out to any SVP member and see where the journey takes you!!

Finally, as always, please remain aware of need around you and just [contact the SVP](../../pages-output/contact/) if you think that we can be of help. We'll always do our best.

God Bless.
