This weekend marks the first Sunday of the new month, so we have some regular SVP activities to remind you of:

Firstly, our shop, known as 'Gifted', is open this weekend and we hope that you find something prayerful there, for you or for your family or friends. Always ask us if there is something in particular that you are looking for and we will try and source it for you.

Secondly, our Bereavement support group will be in the hall on Wednesday at 10:30am, if you feel you need to talk to someone or if you simply want to enjoy some human interaction over a cup of tea or coffee and a biscuit or two.

These are just two of the many workstreams that we operate and if you would like to know more then please just [check our website](https://svpsouthruislip.org.uk) or [speak to any of us](../../pages-output/contact/) around and about.

Finally, as always, please remain aware of need around you and just contact the SVP if you think that we can be of help. We'll always do our best.

God Bless.
