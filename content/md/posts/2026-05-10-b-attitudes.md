Our parish is blessed to have a young persons' SVP Group, officially known as the B-Attitudes, who do all sorts of amazing voluntary work in our community. Mentored by members of our adult SVP team, the B-Attitudes help at the food bank, with litter-picking, with fundraising for the homeless and many other initiatives. We wish all our B-Attitudes the best of luck with any upcoming exams and we look forward to further mentoring during the summer holidays!

If any young people, 14 to 18 years old, have free time after the exams, and want to do something useful, then why not get in touch!!

Finally, as always, please remain aware of need around you and just [contact the SVP](../../pages-output/contact/) if you think that we can be of help. We'll always do our best.

God Bless.
