Our SVP group, formally called a 'Conference', was 10 years old in April! This means that some of our earliest workstreams, such as the food bank, are also 10 years old! Need is ever-present in our society and we see this each Saturday at the food bank. Without your ongoing support we could not have kept the food bank open and we are ever grateful for this. Our supplies are particularly challenged at the moment and, for the first time in our 10 years, we are having to send our clients away empty handed, directing them to other help.

If you are able to donate then please consider doing so and if the donations box in the church porch is full then you can always bring your donations to us, in the hall, at 11am each Saturday as we are setting up the food bank.

Thank you for reading this appeal and we know that the Holy Spirit will do the rest!

And finally, as always, please remain aware of need around you and just [contact the SVP](../../pages-output/contact/) if you think that we can be of help. We'll always do our best.

God Bless.
