Thank you to everyone who supported our fundraising shop, known as 'Gifted', last weekend. We hope you found what you were looking for. If you didn't, please ask us and we'll do our best to source it.

With exams coming to an end and school holidays looming, if any young people would like to take up volunteering, ask us about it. Volunteering is a great character-building opportunity and will strengthen any personal statement you may need to write in the future. Have a think, and if the idea grabs you, please get in touch with any of us.

Finally, as always, please keep an eye on neighbours who may be vulnerable, and [contact the SVP](../../pages-output/contact/) if you think we can help. We'll always do our best.

God bless.
