The school holidays are just around the corner, so many young people will have time on their hands over the coming weeks. If you are between 11 and 18 years of age and are looking for a new hobby, new friends, or new experiences, consider joining our B-Attitudes team. The team meets monthly in the church hall. They come up with ideas for helping those in need in our community and then set about putting those ideas into action.

If this kind of group interests you, please contact the SVP or chat to any of us. You'll always see some of us at the food bank each Saturday. Helping us there is an example of what the B-Attitudes do. (Members under 14 can also help at the food bank, but they need to be accompanied by a parent or guardian.)

Put your thinking caps on, bring your ideas, and get in touch -- we look forward to hearing from you!

Finally, as always, please remain aware of those in need in our community and [contact us](../../pages-output/contact/) if you think the SVP can help. We'll always do our best.

God bless.
