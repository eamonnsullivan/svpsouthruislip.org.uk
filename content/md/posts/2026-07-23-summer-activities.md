It's the time of year when daily school and work routines take a break
for the summer holidays, but please remember that the SVP team is
always around and on standby to help.

Our regular activities remain in place: the food bank is open every
Saturday. Our fundraising shop, 'Gifted', will be open next weekend
(Aug. 1-2). Our bereavement support group meets on the first
Wednesday of the month (Aug. 5) at 10:30am. We also have other
regular activities that may not be so visible, such as home and care
home visits to the lonely and vulnerable.

If you need our help, or if you would like to join us in this work,
please do not hesitate to chat with any of us when you see us. You're
guaranteed to find a listening ear!

Finally, as always, please remain aware of needs in our community and
just [contact us](../../pages-output/contact/) if you think the SVP
can be of help. We'll always do our best.

God bless.
